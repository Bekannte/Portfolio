Terrors and Sweet Dreams
My work inspired by Taylor Swift's new album Midnights, The concept is a journey through terrors and sweet dreams.
Project Details / Background
It’s a momentary glimmer of distraction. The tiniest notion of reminiscent thought that wanders off into wondering, the spark that lights a tinderbox of fixation. And now it is irreversible. The flame has caught. You’re wide awake. Late at night we always vacillate between fantasy and disgust. One moment, your life looks like a night sky of gleaming stars. The next moment a lavender haze has descended. I used particle changes to show my eight dreams, with corresponding music to enrich the form.
